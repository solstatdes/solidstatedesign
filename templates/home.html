About

Solid State Design is Dr. Rob Treharne. A working physicist with an eye for design and a thirst for code.

Rob has a PhD in Renewable Energy and a background in Photovoltaics. He founded Solid State Design this year and is on the hunt for interesting and technical web projects that will further hone his skills and portfolio. He also loves graphic design and is great at designing logos.
