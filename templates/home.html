About

Solid State Design is Dr. Rob Treharne. A working physicisist with an eye for design and a thirst for code.

Rob has a PhD in Renewable Energy and a background in Photovoltaics. He founded Solid State Design this year and is on the hunt for interesting and technical web projects that will further hone his skills and portfolio.
